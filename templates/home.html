About The Project

This DeepFake Detection tool uses MesoNet, a deep learning model specifically designed to detect facial manipulations in images. The model analyzes the mesoscopic properties of images to identify inconsistencies that are typically present in deepfakes. With high accuracy, our tool can help verify the authenticity of facial images in this era of widespread digital manipulation.

How It Works

  1. Upload your image through our simple interface
  2. Our AI model analyzes the image using the MesoNet architecture
  3. Get instant results showing whether the image is likely real or fake
  4. View confidence scores to understand the reliability of the prediction
